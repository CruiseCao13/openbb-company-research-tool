# Limitations

## Not Investment Advice

This project does not provide buy/sell recommendations, target prices, trading signals, or portfolio allocation instructions.

## Data Quality

The project uses OpenBB and yfinance-based data. Free/public financial data may be delayed, incomplete, inconsistent, or wrong.

Always verify important numbers with:

- SEC 10-K / 10-Q
- Company Investor Relations
- Earnings releases
- Official financial statements

## ETF Limitation

Company financial statement analysis may not apply to ETFs, funds, or index products.

For ETFs, analyze:

- Expense ratio
- Holdings
- Index methodology
- Sector exposure
- Country exposure
- Tracking error
- Liquidity

## Prediction Limitation

The tool cannot reliably predict future stock prices.

It can support evidence-based judgment, but it cannot guarantee outcomes.
