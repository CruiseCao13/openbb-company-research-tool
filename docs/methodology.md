# Methodology

## Purpose

This tool standardizes the first layer of company research.

It produces structured data for human analysis. It does not produce investment conclusions.

## Benchmark Comparison

The tool compares a target ticker against a chosen benchmark.

Default benchmark:

```text
SPY
```

Other examples:

- VOO
- QQQ
- IWM
- Another individual stock

The goal is to measure opportunity cost.

## Price Metrics

- Total Return
- CAGR
- 1Y / 6M / 3M Return
- Max Drawdown
- Annualized Volatility
- Sharpe Ratio
- Sortino Ratio
- Calmar Ratio
- Beta
- Alpha
- Correlation
- Tracking Error
- Information Ratio
- Upside Capture
- Downside Capture

## Fundamental Metrics

- Revenue CAGR
- Latest Revenue Growth
- Gross Margin
- Operating Margin
- Net Margin
- FCF Margin
- Positive Net Income Years
- Positive FCF Years

## Research Score

The score combines:

- Growth Score
- Profitability Score
- Quality Trend Score
- Risk Control Score
- Benchmark Score
- Valuation Sanity Score

It is a research-priority heuristic, not a valuation model and not a prediction.

## Final Rule

Use this tool to ask better questions, not to outsource judgment.
