# Valuation Method Fit

Checklist:

- Stable profitable compounders can use earnings and FCF multiples if data supports them.
- Banks require book value, ROE, credit quality, NIM, and capital context.
- REITs require FFO/AFFO, occupancy, leverage, and maturity schedule.
- Biotech and early-stage aerospace often require cash runway, pipeline/project milestones, financing need, and probability-weighted execution framing.
- Negative earnings or unstable cash flow should not be forced into a normal PE conclusion.
- Price history does not prove cheap or expensive valuation.
