# A-Share Accounting Rules

A-share reports should use natural Chinese financial language and RMB units.

Required terms when data is available:

- 营业收入
- 归母净利润
- 扣非净利润
- 经营现金流
- 货币资金
- 有息负债
- 应收账款
- 存货
- 研发费用
- 资产负债率
- ROE

Do not describe A-share companies as 10-K, 10-Q, or SEC filing businesses unless discussing cross-listed disclosures with evidence.

## Sector-Specific A-share Notes

Battery and new energy manufacturing:

- Use 营业收入, 毛利率, 净利率, 经营现金流, 存货, 应收账款, 研发费用, capex, and capacity expansion together.
- If customer concentration, overseas revenue, product mix, or battery-material cost data is missing, mark it as a data gap.

Insurance and integrated financials:

- Use 保费收入, 承保结果, 投资收益, 偿付能力, 资本充足, 准备金, and asset-liability risk when available.
- Industrial free cash flow and net debt / EBITDA are not core insurance metrics.

Pharma and innovative drug:

- Use 药品组合, 研发费用, pipeline / approval progress, 医保/集采 pressure, patent risk, and competition risk.
- Do not treat an established pharma company as only an early-stage biotech cash-runway case.

Mining and nonferrous metals:

- Use 金/铜等金属价格, 产量, 成本曲线, 经营现金流, capex, debt, reserves, mine life, FX, and jurisdiction risk.
- Do not infer durable earnings from one commodity-cycle peak period.
