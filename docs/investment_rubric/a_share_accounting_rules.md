# A-Share Accounting Rules

A-share reports should use natural Chinese financial language and RMB units.

Required terms when data is available:

- 营业收入
- 归母净利润
- 扣非净利润
- 经营现金流
- 货币资金
- 有息负债
- 应收账款
- 存货
- 研发费用
- 资产负债率
- ROE

Do not describe A-share companies as 10-K, 10-Q, or SEC filing businesses unless discussing cross-listed disclosures with evidence.
