# Financial Statement Analysis

Checklist:

- Revenue explains scale and direction, not quality by itself.
- Operating income connects revenue to operating leverage and cost structure.
- Net income must be interpreted with non-operating and one-time items in mind.
- Operating cash flow answers whether the business converts accounting earnings into cash.
- Capex and R&D are uses of cash that may be necessary investment or ongoing burden; do not label them without context.
- Debt, cash, and shares must be handled as balance-sheet and capital-allocation facts, not generic risk words.
