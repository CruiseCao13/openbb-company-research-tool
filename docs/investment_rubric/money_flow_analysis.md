# Money Flow Analysis

Money Flow must answer two concrete questions:

1. Where does cash come from?
2. Where does cash go?

Allowed source categories:

- product revenue
- service revenue
- contract or project revenue
- operating cash flow
- financing
- debt issuance
- equity issuance
- asset sales

Allowed use categories:

- cost of revenue
- R&D
- capex
- working capital
- debt repayment
- buybacks
- dividends
- operating losses

If the data does not support a split, write the missing split as a data gap. Do not use generic sentences such as "money comes from revenue when available."
