# Chart Selection Report

The report limits itself to core evidence charts: price/risk path, financial trend, money flow, valuation, and segment/data-gap handling. Charts exist to answer research questions, not to decorate the report.

## Not Charted

- Company profile text is explained in prose.
- Missing segment data becomes a data gap instead of an empty chart.
- Raw provider rows remain locked data and are not pasted into chart surfaces.
