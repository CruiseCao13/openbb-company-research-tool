# Evidence Map

Key report claims are mapped to locked provider sections, chart references, table references, and confidence labels. Concrete numeric facts must come from locked data; AI interpretation must carry boundaries and confidence.

## Claim Categories

- locked_data_supported: direct provider/calculated evidence.
- AI_interpretation: bounded reasoning from locked data and company profile.
- assumption: explicitly marked hypothesis.
- data_gap: unavailable evidence that blocks stronger claims.
- unsupported: should remain empty; non-empty requires review.
